ArtX aims to bridge the "art" and "tech" communities. We currently exist at Stanford University.

Art X GRID X MINT Artist in Residence Program

This is our inaugural "Artist in Residence" program, bringing together artists from different engineering disciplines to create works of art to be projected onto a 20ft x 30ft laser and water mist lattice surface erected by GRID in front of the Anderson Collection for two weeks during the end of May and beginning of June alongside internationally acclaimed artists, culminating in an ArtX only GRID exhibition on June 8th. Themes for this exhibition will be Frankenstein, Cartesian, Entropy, +++, and Anatomy.
